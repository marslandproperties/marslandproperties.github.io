About Gowtham Estates

Gowtham Estates, developed and marketed by B Square Developers, is a premium residential layout situated in the prime location of Sakthi Nagar, KK Nagar, Trichy. Our vision is to create sustainable, well-planned residential spaces that offer quality living for families.

The layout is meticulously planned with wide roads, proper infrastructure, and all essential amenities. Every plot is designed to provide optimal space utilization, excellent ventilation, and a green environment.

With clear titles, legal approvals, and transparent dealings, we ensure complete peace of mind for our customers. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction makes us the preferred choice for home buyers in Trichy.
